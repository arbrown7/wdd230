Newtown Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Promoting economic growth and local businesses in Newtown, PA

Site Name: Newtown Chamber of Commerce

This site represents the Chamber of Commerce for the town of Newtown, PA. The name was selected to clearly convey the purpose of the site, which is to serve as a hub for local businesses and community engagement. 'Chamber' is included to signify the site's association with the local business community.

Site Purpose

The Newtown Chamber of Commerce website serves two main purposes:

  • To promote local businesses and economic development by offering member services, networking opportunities, and community events.
  • To provide a central hub of information for residents, visitors, and businesses, offering resources, event information, and contact details for the Chamber's leadership.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events can I attend to network with other local business owners?
  • Where can I find contact information for the Chamber's leadership team?
  • What resources are available for new businesses looking to set up in Newtown?

Color Schema

The color scheme selected for the Newtown Chamber of Commerce website reflects the local, traditional feel of the town while maintaining professionalism:

  • #46534E (Medium Dark Greenish-Gray): Used for headers, footer background, and accents to convey trust and stability.
  • #313a37 (Dark Greenish-Gray): Used for text on white background.
  • #FFFFFF (White): Used for the main background and content sections for a clean, professional look that ensures readability.
  • #dfe7e9 (Very Light Grayish-Blue): Used for secondary background colors.

Typography

The Montserrat font was chosen for the site due to its modern, clean lines and excellent readability:

  • Montserrat Bold (700): Used for headers such as <h1> and <h2> elements to draw attention to important content.
  • Montserrat Regular (400): Used for body text to ensure the site remains easy to read on both desktop and mobile devices.
